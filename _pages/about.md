Has a background in Environmental Engineering. Is a specialist in Geoprocessing and MSc in Remote Sensing. Currently is Ph.D. Candidate in Remote Sensing at the National Institute for Space Research - INPE. Studies the impact of fragmentation, mainly the edge effect, on the carbon stocks within the tropical forests. Your main interests are Land-use and Land-cover Dynamics, Fire Dynamics in Tropical Ecosystems, Environmental Changes in the Tropics, Carbon Cycle and Remote Sensing of Vegetation..
